Directions

We are located in Engineering Gateway 3151 (building 321 on the standard, printable campus map). On this interactive map, type Engineering Gateway in the search box to find us. The closest parking structure is the Anteater Parking structure, which is just to the right (east) on the map, at the corner of East Peltason and Anteater Drive. On this map mouse over engineering, and the parking garage is highlighted in yellow (identified as "recommended"). If you pre-arranged a parking slot with Dr. Reinkensmeyer, you can give your name to the person at the parking booth at the entrance to the garage, and you should be on a list. Prof. Reinkensmeyer's office is in Engineering Gateway 3225, phone 949-824-5218.
